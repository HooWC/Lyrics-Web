Cyberbullying 网络暴力

木已 Muyi

身心疲惫，不想再管其他事情，我只想一个人呆着。

I'm feeling exhausted, don't want to deal with anything else, I just want to be alone.

身心疲惫，不想再管其他事情，我只想一个人呆着。

The pressure's mounting, and I can't take it anymore, My mind's constantly thinking about how to get through these tough days.

压力越来越大，什么也不想再做，满脑子一直在思考如何度过艰难的日子。

Every day is a struggle, I can't relax, I have so much to do, no time to play.

每一天都不能放松，我还有很多事情要做，没空陪你们玩。

Money's run out, I need to hustle, Whether it's through legal means or not, I'll do what it takes.

钱也用完了，我需要努力去赚钱，不管是黑钱还是死人钱。

I've always envied others, why am I so unlucky?

我一直羡慕其他人，为什么我总是那么倒霉？

If you're not mute, please speak up, Don't become that person I despise.

如果你不是哑巴，请你说说话，不要变成那个我讨厌的人。

My wallet was stolen, what more can you ask of me? I'm just a young person, but I understand all the lessons.

钱包被偷走，你还要我怎么样，我只是个年轻人，却明白所有的道理。

It feels like a knife has slashed through me, That sensation is so painful.

就像一把刀斩了我一样，那感觉十分的痛苦。


Hey, what is cyberbullying? It's the act of cursing others online,

哟，网络暴力是什么？是在网络上诅咒他人的行为。

They never think about how others feel, it's always about themselves.

他们根本没有想过其他人的感受，只总是在乎自己。

Their hearts are like demons, living only for money, Their eyes all gleam with gold.

他们的内心是魔鬼，只为钱而活着，他们双眼都是黄金色。

They can kill others for money, and their relentless verbal violence, keeps attacking him

为了钱可以杀了其他人，不停歇的语言暴力一直攻击他。

And that "him" is me. Their hands are like machine guns, never stopping,

那个他就是我自己啦，他们的手就像机关枪一样不停歇。

Whether it's cursing or wishing me dead, they think they're righteous, it's laughable!

不管是诅咒还是祝福我去死的话，他们以为自己是正义，笑死人了！

Would justice wish death upon others? They're just brainless kids.

正义还会祝福别人去死吗，他们都是没脑子的小屁孩而已。

One day, I'll send these people to a mental hospital, And make them regret the words they once said.

总有一天我要将这些人送去精神病院，让他们后悔自己曾经说过的话。


No one cares about me, I'm always alone, please don't bother with me.

没有人会在乎我，我总是一个人呆着，请不要管我。

The feeling of loneliness, the feeling of sadness, it's all...

孤独的感觉，悲伤的感觉，所有...

Please don't hurt me anymore, I just want a peaceful day.

请你们不要再伤害我了，我只是想要一个平静的日子。

I've turned off all my computers and phones, not looking at any of the hateful comments online.

我关掉所有的电脑和手机，不看网络上所有骂我的评论。

I'm scared, I might not be able to take it and consider suicide.

我害怕，我会受不了去自杀。

Life is so fragile, please stop your current actions.

生命是如此的渺小，请你们停下你们现在的行为。

Cyberbullying is terrifying, please don't attack anymore.

网络暴力很恐怖，请不要再攻击了。

These hurts are unforgettable, please don't do this, don't do this anymore...

这些伤害是遗忘不了了，请你们不要再这样了，不要再这样了...


I don't want to keep living like this; what's the point of a life without love?

我不想再这样过着日子，没有爱的生活还有什么意义？

Turning the page, it's the same answer again: no one will look at you, and this world is gray.

翻开下一页还是一样的答案，没有人会看你一眼，这个世界是灰色的。

I should have left this city long ago to find the home I want, no need to pretend to be a good person anymore.

早就应该离开这个城市去寻找自己想要的家，不用再假装自己是好人了。

Pressure, sadness, pressure, sadness, pressure, sadness.

压力、悲伤，压力、悲伤，压力、悲伤。

I apologize for my social behavior; none of you are what I desire.

为社交说了抱歉，你们都不是我想要的样子。

You hung up my call, the computer screen dimmed, and it feels like I'm back in my lonely times.

你挂断了我的电话，电脑没有了亮度，仿佛又回到孤独的时候。

Is where you are now any good? The words I can't say are forever hidden in my heart,

你现在在的地方好不好？说不出的话永远藏在我的心里。

But you always say to me, "Forget it."

但你总是对我说“算了”。


Some say: Life is meant to be lived for yourself, don't concern yourself with those who hurt you.

有人说：生活就是要为自己而活，不要去理会那些伤害你的人。

They aren't qualified to criticize you; you are unique; you must believe in yourself.

他们没资格去批评你，你是唯一的，你要相信你自己。

You are the strongest; you are loved by someone; there's no right or wrong, be brave and act.

你是最强的，你是有人爱的，没有对和错，勇敢去做。

Don't always doubt yourself, don't fear the future, don't fear losing.

不要总是怀疑自己，不要害怕面对未来，不要害怕失去。

Don't hang your head low; be confident, young one.

不要低下你的头，自信点，年轻人。

Don't forget your purpose, instead of being hurt by cyberbullying and losing your life.

不要忘了你的目的是什么，而不是被这些网络暴力伤害，然后失去了生命

Everything is fleeting; cherish it; don't waste your time on those people.

所有的一切，都很短暂，你要好好去珍惜，不要浪费时间在那些人身上
